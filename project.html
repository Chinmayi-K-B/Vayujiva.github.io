Problem Statement

The project addresses the following challenges:

  • Efficient allocation of resources in a decentralized city structure.
  • Minimizing environmental impact while maximizing urban livability.
  • Integrating smart technologies for seamless city management.

Business Cases Identified

We have identified key business opportunities within the project:

  • Smart energy management systems to reduce utility costs.
  • Intelligent transportation networks for efficient mobility.
  • IoT-enabled waste management for a cleaner city.
  • Green building initiatives promoting sustainable development.
