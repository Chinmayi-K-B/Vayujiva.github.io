Problem Statement

Urban mobility faces issues like traffic congestion, lack of parking, and slow emergency response. A smart system that manages traffic, parking, and emergencies can improve efficiency and reduce delays.

Project Description

Project Description: Vayujiva Vayujiva is a groundbreaking initiative envisioning a sustainable and futuristic smart city. Rooted in eco-friendly principles, Vayujiva focuses on decentralization, resource efficiency, and community well-being, offering an urban solution that is both innovative and inclusive. Key features of Vayujiva include: - Decentralized Development**: Balanced facilities and equal focus on every part of the city. - Smart Technology Integration**: Advanced solutions for energy management, waste reduction, and city-wide connectivity. - Eco-Conscious Infrastructure**: Renewable energy, sustainable construction, and green spaces. - People-Centric Design**: Inclusive spaces for healthcare, education, and recreation, promoting social harmony. Vayujiva addresses the pressing challenges of overpopulation, resource depletion, and environmental sustainability, presenting a vision for smarter, greener cities of the future.

SL NO Business Case Identified Description Owner Problem Tools
1 Real-time Traffic Monitoring Collect, process, and store traffic data in real-time using IoT devices and sensors. Ananya B P Arrays, Hashing, Sparse Table
2 Citizen Feedback Processing Collect, analyze, and act on citizen feedback regarding traffic issues. Ria Brute Force String Search, Rabin-Karp, Lookup Table

6 EV Charging Station Management Locate and manage EV charging stations in the city. Ananya and Chinmayi Binary Search Tree, Trie, Arrays
