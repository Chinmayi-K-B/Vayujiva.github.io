Business Ideas

1. Smart Parking Systems

Aspect Details
Problem
  • Urban Congestion: Limited parking spaces causing traffic and pollution.
  • Inefficient Parking Utilization: Underutilized spaces and increased search time.
  • Environmental Impact: Excessive fuel consumption and emissions.
Solution Smart parking systems using tries and dynamic queues for real-time slot management.
Applications Trie for Slot Availability: Efficient storage and retrieval of slots based on location.
Dynamic Queue: Real-time vehicle slot allocation.

2. EV Charging Station Management

Aspect Details
Problem
  • Growing EV adoption requires efficient charging management.
  • Need for sustainable and accessible infrastructure.
Solution Smart management using tries and BSTs for real-time allocation of EV charging slots.
Applications Trie for Charging Slot Availability: Quick lookup of available stations and slots.
Binary Search Tree (BST): Real-time slot allocation and deallocation.
