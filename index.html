About the Course

What is this course about?

The Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) course explores how algorithms and data structures shape the core of computer science and beyond. This course connects theory to practice, helping students solve complex problems in areas like business, science, and technology. Students will learn how nature-inspired processes influence algorithm design, sharpening analytical thinking and equipping them with tools for creative problem-solving.

About Our Project

Our Vision for the Future

Vayujiva is a visionary smart city designed to be self-sustaining, environmentally conscious, and technologically advanced. Our project focuses on creating a city where infrastructure integrates renewable energy, efficient transportation, and green spaces. The design emphasizes sustainability and user-friendly urban planning to meet the needs of future generations. With our innovative approach to city planning, we aim to build a city that is a model for future urban living.
