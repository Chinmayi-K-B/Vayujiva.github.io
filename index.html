About the Course

What is the course about?

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) is not just a part of computer science; it's at the core of problem-solving across various domains. The course connects nature's patterns with algorithmic solutions and emphasizes analytical thinking, equipping students with problem-solving skills for real-world challenges.

What kind of data structures and algorithms have you studied?

We explored arrays, strings, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs, and skip lists. Each data structure reflects a real-world concept, helping to efficiently manage and process information for various applications, from web pages to social networks.

How do you connect the course with real-time applications?

Algorithms are embedded in everyday life, optimizing experiences like GPS routing and product recommendations. They simplify complex tasks, making technology smarter, safer, and more intuitive, seamlessly shaping how we interact with the digital world.
