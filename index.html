About the course

What is the course about?

- Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) is not just a branch of computer science; it is the very foundation of the field. Its principles extend beyond computer science and have applications in various domains, including science, business, and technology. This course focuses on recognizing the inherent patterns found in nature, as many natural processes inspire computational algorithms, and vice versa. It fosters analytical thinking, equipping students with problem-solving strategies applicable to a wide range of real-world challenges.

What kind of data structures and algorithms have you studied?
